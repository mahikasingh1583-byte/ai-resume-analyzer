Computer Vision Engineer

- Strong Python programming skills
- Experience with OpenCV and image processing
- Deep knowledge of CNN architectures (ResNet, YOLO, EfficientNet)
- Experience with PyTorch or TensorFlow for vision tasks
- Knowledge of object detection, segmentation, and classification
- Familiarity with data augmentation techniques
- Experience with model optimization (quantization, pruning)
- Understanding of camera systems and image acquisition
- Experience with annotation tools and dataset creation

Nice to have:
- Experience with 3D vision and point clouds
- Knowledge of video understanding models
- Familiarity with edge deployment (NVIDIA Jetson, OpenVINO)
- Experience with medical imaging
- Knowledge of generative models (GANs, Diffusion)
- Experience with AR/VR applications

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement computer vision pipelines
- Train and evaluate vision models
- Optimize models for production deployment
- Collaborate with hardware and robotics teams
- Build data collection and annotation workflows

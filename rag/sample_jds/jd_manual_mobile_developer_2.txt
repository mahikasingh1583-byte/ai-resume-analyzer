Mobile Developer (React Native)

About the Role:
We're looking for a Mobile Developer to help build and maintain our cross-platform mobile app, used by over 1 million active users on iOS and Android.

Responsibilities:
- Develop and maintain features in our React Native application
- Optimize app performance and troubleshoot platform-specific issues
- Collaborate with backend engineers to integrate REST/GraphQL APIs
- Write unit and integration tests to ensure code quality
- Participate in app store release processes for iOS and Android

Requirements:
- 3+ years of experience building production mobile apps with React Native
- Strong JavaScript/TypeScript skills
- Experience with native modules and bridging (Swift/Kotlin knowledge a plus)
- Familiarity with state management (Redux, MobX, or Context API)
- Experience publishing apps to the App Store and Google Play

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with Expo
- Understanding of mobile CI/CD (Fastlane, Bitrise)
- Experience with push notifications and deep linking

Benefits:

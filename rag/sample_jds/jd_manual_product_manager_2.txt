Product Manager (B2B SaaS)

About the Role:
We're hiring a Product Manager to own the roadmap for one of our core product lines. You'll work closely with engineering, design, and customer success to ship features that solve real customer problems.

Responsibilities:
- Define and prioritize product roadmap based on customer feedback and business goals
- Write clear product requirements and user stories
- Partner with engineering and design throughout the development lifecycle
- Analyze product usage data to identify opportunities for improvement
- Communicate roadmap and progress to stakeholders and leadership

Requirements:
- 3+ years of product management experience, ideally in B2B SaaS
- Strong analytical skills; comfortable working with SQL and product analytics tools (Amplitude, Mixpanel)
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Experience running user research and translating insights into requirements
- Ability to balance technical constraints with business priorities

Preferred:
- Experience with Agile/Scrum methodologies
- Background in a technical role (engineering, data) prior to PM
- Experience with tools like Jira, Productboard, or Linear

What We Offer:
- Competitive salary + equity
- Remote-first culture
- Flexible PTO
- Annual company retreat

Scrum Master

We are looking for an experienced Scrum Master to lead our Agile teams.

Requirements:
- 2+ years of Scrum Master experience
- Certified Scrum Master (CSM or PSM) certification
- Strong knowledge of Agile and Scrum methodologies
- Experience facilitating sprint planning, retrospectives, and daily standups
- Ability to remove impediments and coach teams
- Experience with project management tools (Jira, Confluence, Trello)
- Strong communication and leadership skills
- Knowledge of Kanban and Lean principles
- Experience working with distributed teams
- Understanding of software development processes

Nice to have:
- SAFe Agile certification
- Experience with multiple Agile frameworks (XP, Crystal)
- Knowledge of DevOps practices
- Experience coaching leadership on Agile transformation
- Familiarity with OKRs and goal-setting frameworks
- Technical background in software development

Responsibilities:
- Facilitate Agile ceremonies and events
- Coach team members on Scrum practices
- Remove blockers and impediments for the team
- Track and report sprint metrics and velocity
- Support product owner in backlog refinement
- Drive continuous improvement initiatives

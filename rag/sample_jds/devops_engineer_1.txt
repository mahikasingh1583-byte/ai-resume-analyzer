DevOps Engineer

We are looking for a DevOps Engineer to streamline our development and deployment processes.

Requirements:
- 3+ years of DevOps or Site Reliability Engineering experience
- Strong experience with Docker and Kubernetes
- Proficiency with AWS, Azure, or GCP
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines (Jenkins, GitHub Actions, GitLab CI)
- Knowledge of Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, Ansible)
- Strong Linux/Unix administration skills
- Experience with monitoring tools (Prometheus, Grafana, ELK Stack)
- Scripting skills in Python, Bash, or Go
- Knowledge of networking concepts (DNS, TCP/IP, load balancing)
- Experience with Git and version control workflows

Nice to have:
- Kubernetes certification (CKA or CKAD)
- AWS certification (Solutions Architect or DevOps Engineer)
- Experience with service mesh (Istio)
- Knowledge of security practices (DevSecOps)
- Experience with Helm charts
- Familiarity with cloud cost optimization

Responsibilities:
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage cloud infrastructure and Kubernetes clusters
- Implement monitoring and alerting systems
- Automate repetitive operational tasks
- Ensure system reliability and uptime
- Collaborate with development teams on deployment strategies

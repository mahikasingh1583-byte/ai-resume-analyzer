Machine Learning Engineer

About the Team:
Our ML team builds and deploys models that power personalized recommendations for over 5 million active users. We're looking for an ML Engineer to help take models from prototype to production.

Responsibilities:
- Build, train, and deploy machine learning models at scale
- Design and maintain data pipelines for model training (Airflow)
- Collaborate with data scientists to productionize research models
- Monitor model performance in production and implement retraining pipelines
- Optimize inference latency for real-time serving

Requirements:
- 3+ years of experience building and deploying ML models in production
- Strong Python skills, including experience with PyTorch or TensorFlow
- Experience with ML pipeline tools (MLflow, Kubeflow, or similar)
- Solid understanding of software engineering best practices (testing, CI/CD)
- Experience with cloud ML infrastructure (AWS SageMaker, GCP Vertex AI)

Nice to Have:
- Experience with recommendation systems or ranking models
- Familiarity with feature stores (Feast, Tecton)
- Experience with LLMs or vector databases

Compensation & Benefits:
- $130,000-$175,000 base salary
- Equity compensation
- Remote-first with quarterly in-person summits
- Full health benefits + 401(k) matching

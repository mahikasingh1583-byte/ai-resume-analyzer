Embedded Systems Engineer

Join our hardware team to develop firmware and embedded software.

Requirements:
- 3+ years of embedded systems development experience
- Strong C and C++ programming skills
- Experience with microcontrollers (ARM Cortex, STM32, ESP32)
- Knowledge of RTOS (FreeRTOS, Zephyr)
- Experience with communication protocols (UART, SPI, I2C, CAN)
- Familiarity with PCB design and schematic reading
- Knowledge of debugging tools (JTAG, oscilloscopes, logic analyzers)
- Experience with bootloader development
- Understanding of memory management in embedded systems
- Knowledge of low-power design techniques

Nice to have:
- Experience with Linux kernel development
- Knowledge of wireless protocols (BLE, WiFi, LoRa)
- Familiarity with AUTOSAR for automotive applications
- Experience with hardware bring-up
- Knowledge of safety standards (IEC 61508, ISO 26262)
- Experience with FPGA development (VHDL, Verilog)

Responsibilities:
- Develop firmware for embedded hardware platforms
- Implement and optimize device drivers
- Collaborate with hardware engineers during board bring-up
- Debug and resolve hardware-software interface issues
- Write technical documentation for firmware systems
- Conduct code reviews and ensure coding standards

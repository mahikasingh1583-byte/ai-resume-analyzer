Product Manager

We are seeking a Product Manager to lead the development of our core products.

Requirements:
- 3+ years of product management experience
- Strong analytical and data-driven decision-making skills
- Experience with product roadmap planning and prioritization
- Knowledge of Agile and Scrum methodologies
- Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills
- Experience with user research and customer discovery
- Familiarity with product analytics tools (Mixpanel, Amplitude, Google Analytics)
- Understanding of software development processes
- Experience writing detailed product requirements and user stories
- Strong leadership and cross-functional collaboration skills

Nice to have:
- Technical background in software engineering
- Experience with A/B testing platforms
- Knowledge of UX design principles
- Familiarity with SQL for data analysis
- Experience with project management tools (Jira, Asana, Linear)
- MBA or relevant advanced degree

Responsibilities:
- Define product vision strategy and roadmap
- Gather and prioritize product requirements from stakeholders
- Work closely with engineering and design teams
- Analyze product metrics and user feedback
- Drive product launches and go-to-market strategies
- Communicate product updates to leadership and teams

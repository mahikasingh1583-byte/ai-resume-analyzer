Data Analyst

Job Summary:
We're looking for a Data Analyst to join our growing analytics team. You'll partner closely with marketing, product, and finance teams to turn raw data into actionable insight.

Key Responsibilities:
- Build and maintain dashboards in Tableau/Looker to track key business metrics
- Write complex SQL queries to extract and analyze data from our warehouse (Snowflake)
- Partner with stakeholders to define KPIs and reporting requirements
- Conduct ad-hoc analysis to support product and marketing decisions
- Identify trends, anomalies, and opportunities in large datasets

Requirements:
- 2+ years of experience in a data analyst or business analyst role
- Strong SQL skills (window functions, CTEs, query optimization)
- Experience with a BI tool such as Tableau, Looker, or Power BI
- Working knowledge of Python or R for data manipulation (pandas preferred)
- Excellent communication skills — able to explain data insights to non-technical stakeholders

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with dbt or other data transformation tools
- Familiarity with A/B testing methodology and statistics
- Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Economics, Computer Science, or related field

What We Offer:
- Hybrid work model (3 days in office)
- Comprehensive health benefits
- Annual performance bonus
- Professional development budget

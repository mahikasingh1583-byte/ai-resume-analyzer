Mobile App Developer

We are looking for a Mobile Developer to build cross-platform mobile applications.

- 2+ years of mobile development experience
- Proficiency in React Native or Flutter
- Knowledge of iOS development (Swift) or Android development (Kotlin)
- Experience with mobile UI/UX design principles
- Familiarity with REST API integration
- Knowledge of mobile app state management
- Experience with app store submission (Apple App Store, Google Play)
- Understanding of mobile app performance optimization
- Familiarity with push notifications and background services
- Experience with Git and version control

Nice to have:
- Experience with both iOS and Android native development
- Knowledge of mobile security best practices
- Familiarity with Firebase for mobile backends
- Experience with mobile testing frameworks
- Knowledge of deep linking and app navigation patterns
- Experience with Bluetooth or IoT integrations

Responsibilities:
- Design and build mobile applications for iOS and Android
- Integrate with backend APIs and services
- Ensure app performance and responsiveness
- Write unit and UI tests
- Collaborate with designers to implement UI
- Publish and maintain apps on app stores

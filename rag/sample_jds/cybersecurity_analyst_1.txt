Cybersecurity Analyst

We are seeking a Cybersecurity Analyst to protect our organization's digital assets.

Requirements:
- 2+ years of experience in cybersecurity or information security
- Knowledge of network security protocols (TCP/IP, DNS, SSL/TLS)
- Experience with SIEM tools (Splunk, IBM QRadar, Microsoft Sentinel)
- Understanding of cybersecurity frameworks (NIST, ISO 27001, CIS)
- Experience with vulnerability assessment tools (Nessus, OpenVAS)
- Knowledge of penetration testing concepts
- Familiarity with incident response procedures
- Understanding of firewall management and intrusion detection systems
- Knowledge of threat intelligence platforms
- Security certifications preferred (CompTIA Security+, CEH, CISSP)

Nice to have:
- Experience with cloud security (AWS Security, Azure Security Center)
- Knowledge of OWASP Top 10
- Familiarity with digital forensics tools
- Experience with SOC operations
- Knowledge of malware analysis
- Experience with Python for security automation

Responsibilities:
- Monitor security systems for threats and vulnerabilities
- Conduct vulnerability assessments and penetration tests
- Respond to security incidents and breaches
- Develop security policies and procedures
- Perform security audits and compliance checks
- Train staff on cybersecurity best practices

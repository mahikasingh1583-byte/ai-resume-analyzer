Cloud Infrastructure Engineer (AWS)

Overview:
We're expanding our cloud infrastructure team to support rapid growth. You'll be responsible for designing, building, and maintaining our AWS environment across multiple regions.

Responsibilities:
- Architect and maintain scalable AWS infrastructure (EC2, ECS, Lambda, RDS)
- Implement Infrastructure as Code using Terraform
- Ensure security best practices across cloud environments (IAM, VPC, security groups)
- Optimize cloud costs and monitor usage
- Support disaster recovery and business continuity planning

Requirements:
- 3+ years of hands-on AWS experience
- Strong knowledge of Terraform or CloudFormation
- Experience with networking concepts (VPC, subnets, load balancers)
- Scripting experience in Python or Bash
- Understanding of security best practices in cloud environments

Preferred Qualifications:
- AWS Certified Solutions Architect (Associate or Professional)
- Experience with multi-account AWS organizations
- Familiarity with container orchestration (ECS or Kubernetes)

What We Offer:
- Fully remote position
- Competitive salary + annual bonus
- Home office setup budget
- Generous PTO policy

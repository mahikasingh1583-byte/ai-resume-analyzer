Data Scientist

We are seeking a talented Data Scientist to extract insights from complex datasets.

Requirements:
- 3+ years of experience in data science or machine learning
- Strong proficiency in Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn)
- Experience with machine learning algorithms (regression, classification, clustering)
- Knowledge of deep learning frameworks (TensorFlow or PyTorch)
- Strong SQL skills for data extraction and manipulation
- Experience with data visualization tools (Matplotlib, Seaborn, Tableau)
- Familiarity with statistical analysis and hypothesis testing
- Experience with Jupyter Notebooks
- Knowledge of feature engineering and model evaluation
- Strong communication skills to present findings to stakeholders

Nice to have:
- Experience with NLP and text processing
- Knowledge of computer vision
- Experience with MLflow or similar MLOps tools
- Familiarity with Spark for big data processing
- Knowledge of A/B testing methodologies
- Experience with cloud ML platforms (AWS SageMaker, GCP Vertex AI)

Responsibilities:
- Build and deploy machine learning models
- Analyze large datasets to identify trends and patterns
- Create data pipelines for model training
- Collaborate with engineering teams to productionize models
- Present insights and recommendations to business stakeholders

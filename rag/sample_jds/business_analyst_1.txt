Business Analyst

We are seeking a Business Analyst to bridge the gap between business needs and technology.

Requirements:
- 2+ years of business analysis experience
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Experience with requirements gathering and documentation
- Knowledge of business process modeling (BPMN)
- Proficiency in SQL for data analysis
- Experience with project management tools (Jira, Confluence)
- Familiarity with Agile and Scrum methodologies
- Strong communication and stakeholder management skills
- Experience creating use cases and user stories
- Proficiency in Excel and data visualization

Nice to have:
- Knowledge of ERP systems (SAP, Oracle)
- Experience with process automation (RPA)
- Familiarity with UML diagrams
- Knowledge of financial modeling
- Experience with Tableau or Power BI
- Business Analysis certification (CBAP, PMI-PBA)

Responsibilities:
- Gather and document business requirements
- Analyze business processes and identify improvements
- Facilitate workshops with stakeholders
- Create functional specifications and user stories
- Support UAT and quality assurance processes
- Communicate project progress to stakeholders

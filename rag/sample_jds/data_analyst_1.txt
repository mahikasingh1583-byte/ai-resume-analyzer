Data Analyst

We are looking for a Data Analyst to transform data into actionable business insights.

Requirements:
- 2+ years of data analysis experience
- Strong SQL skills for complex data queries
- Proficiency in Excel and Google Sheets
- Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, or Looker)
- Knowledge of Python or R for data analysis
- Experience with statistical analysis and data modeling
- Familiarity with ETL processes
- Strong analytical and critical thinking skills
- Excellent communication skills to present findings
- Experience working with large datasets

Nice to have:
- Knowledge of Pandas and NumPy
- Experience with Google Analytics or Adobe Analytics
- Familiarity with A/B testing and experimentation
- Knowledge of data warehousing concepts (Snowflake, BigQuery)
- Experience with dbt for data transformation
- Understanding of business intelligence concepts

Responsibilities:
- Analyze large datasets to extract meaningful insights
- Create dashboards and reports for stakeholders
- Identify trends and patterns in business data
- Work with business teams to understand data requirements
- Ensure data quality and accuracy
- Present findings and recommendations to leadership

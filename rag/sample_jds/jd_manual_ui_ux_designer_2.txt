UI/UX Designer

About Us:
We design tools that help creative teams collaborate more effectively. We're looking for a UI/UX Designer to help shape the next generation of our product.

Responsibilities:
- Design intuitive, accessible user interfaces for web and mobile
- Conduct user research and usability testing to validate design decisions
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups in Figma
- Collaborate closely with product managers and engineers throughout the design process
- Maintain and evolve our design system

Requirements:
- 3+ years of experience in UI/UX design for digital products
- Strong portfolio demonstrating end-to-end design process
- Proficiency in Figma (component libraries, auto-layout, prototyping)
- Solid understanding of accessibility standards (WCAG)
- Experience conducting user research and synthesizing findings

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with design systems at scale
- Basic understanding of HTML/CSS to collaborate effectively with engineers
- Experience with motion design or interactive prototyping tools

Benefits:
- Remote-friendly (with quarterly team meetups)
- Health/dental/vision coverage
- $1,000 annual creative tools/software budget
- Flexible working hours

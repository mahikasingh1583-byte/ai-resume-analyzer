DevOps Engineer

Overview:
Our platform team is responsible for the infrastructure that powers our entire product suite. We're looking for a DevOps Engineer to help us improve deployment velocity and system reliability.

Responsibilities:
- Manage and improve CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions/Jenkins)
- Maintain and scale infrastructure on AWS using Terraform
- Monitor system health and respond to incidents (on-call rotation)
- Implement and improve observability (logging, metrics, tracing)
- Collaborate with development teams to improve deployment practices

Requirements:
- 3+ years of experience in a DevOps, SRE, or Infrastructure role
- Strong experience with AWS (EC2, S3, RDS, VPC, IAM)
- Proficiency with Infrastructure as Code (Terraform or CloudFormation)
- Experience with containerization (Docker) and orchestration (Kubernetes)
- Scripting ability in Python or Bash

Preferred:
- Experience with GitOps workflows (ArgoCD, Flux)
- Familiarity with Prometheus/Grafana for monitoring
- Certifications: AWS Solutions Architect or similar

Compensation:
- $120,000-$160,000 base salary
- Equity package
- Fully remote with occasional travel for team offsites

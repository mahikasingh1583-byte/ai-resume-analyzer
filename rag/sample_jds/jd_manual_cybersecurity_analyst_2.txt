Cybersecurity Analyst

Job Summary:
We're looking for a Cybersecurity Analyst to help protect our systems and data as we scale. You'll be part of a small but mighty security team responsible for threat detection, incident response, and compliance.

Responsibilities:
- Monitor security alerts and investigate potential incidents (SIEM tools)
- Conduct vulnerability assessments and coordinate remediation efforts
- Assist with security audits and compliance requirements (SOC 2, ISO 27001)
- Develop and maintain security policies and procedures
- Participate in incident response and post-incident reviews

Requirements:
- 2+ years of experience in a cybersecurity or IT security role
- Familiarity with SIEM tools (Splunk, QRadar, or similar)
- Understanding of common attack vectors and mitigation strategies
- Knowledge of networking fundamentals (TCP/IP, firewalls, VPNs)
- Basic scripting skills (Python or PowerShell) for automation

Preferred Qualifications:
- Security certifications (Security+, CySA+, or working toward CISSP)
- Experience with cloud security (AWS or Azure security tools)
- Familiarity with compliance frameworks (SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR)

Benefits:
- Hybrid work model
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Certification reimbursement program
- 401(k) with company match

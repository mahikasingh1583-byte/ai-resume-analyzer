Data Engineer

About the Role:
We're building a modern data platform to support analytics and machine learning across the company. We need a Data Engineer to help design and scale our data pipelines.

Responsibilities:
- Design, build, and maintain ETL/ELT pipelines using Airflow and dbt
- Manage our data warehouse (Snowflake) and optimize query performance
- Ensure data quality and implement monitoring/alerting for pipeline failures
- Collaborate with data scientists and analysts to make data accessible and reliable
- Document data models and maintain a data catalog

Requirements:
- 3+ years of experience in data engineering
- Strong SQL skills and experience with a cloud data warehouse (Snowflake, BigQuery, or Redshift)
- Proficiency in Python for pipeline development
- Experience with orchestration tools (Airflow, Dagster, or Prefect)
- Understanding of data modeling concepts (star schema, dimensional modeling)

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with dbt for data transformation
- Familiarity with streaming data (Kafka, Kinesis)
- Experience with infrastructure as code (Terraform)

What We Offer:
- Competitive salary and equity
- Remote-first, flexible schedule
- Comprehensive health benefits
- Professional development budget

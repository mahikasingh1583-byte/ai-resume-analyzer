UI/UX Designer

We are looking for a UI/UX Designer to create beautiful and intuitive user experiences.

Requirements:
- 2+ years of UI/UX design experience
- Proficiency in Figma or Adobe XD
- Strong understanding of user-centered design principles
- Experience conducting user research and usability testing
- Knowledge of interaction design and prototyping
- Familiarity with design systems and component libraries
- Understanding of accessibility standards (WCAG)
- Experience with responsive and mobile-first design
- Ability to translate wireframes into high-fidelity designs
- Strong visual design skills (typography, color, layout)

Nice to have:
- Basic knowledge of HTML and CSS
- Experience with motion design (Principle, After Effects)
- Knowledge of design tokens
- Familiarity with Agile development processes
- Experience with user analytics tools
- Portfolio demonstrating end-to-end design process

Responsibilities:
- Create wireframes prototypes and high-fidelity designs
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Collaborate with product managers and developers
- Maintain and evolve the design system
- Present design solutions to stakeholders
- Iterate designs based on user feedback and data

Site Reliability Engineer

We are seeking an SRE to ensure reliability and scalability of our systems.

Requirements:
- 3+ years of SRE or DevOps experience
- Strong software engineering skills (Python, Go, or Java)
- Experience with Kubernetes and container orchestration
- Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Experience with observability tools (Prometheus, Grafana, Jaeger)
- Familiarity with incident management and on-call processes
- Knowledge of SLIs, SLOs, and error budgets
- Experience with chaos engineering principles
- Strong understanding of distributed systems
- Experience with capacity planning and performance tuning

Nice to have:
- Experience with service mesh (Istio, Linkerd)
- Knowledge of eBPF for performance monitoring
- Familiarity with AIOps tools
- Experience with multi-region deployments
- Knowledge of database reliability patterns
- Experience with post-mortem facilitation

Responsibilities:
- Maintain and improve system reliability and uptime
- Build and maintain monitoring and alerting systems
- Respond to and resolve production incidents
- Conduct post-mortems and drive improvements
- Automate operational tasks to reduce toil
- Collaborate with developers on reliability best practices
